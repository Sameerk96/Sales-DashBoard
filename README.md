1. Project Title / Headline
📈 Super Store Sales Dashboard: Sales, Profit & Customer Insights Analysis

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer purchasing behavior, shipping preferences, and regional performance across different product categories and states.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is a comprehensive business intelligence solution developed in Power BI to monitor and analyze sales and profitability across multiple regions, customer segments, product categories, and shipping methods.

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to identify high-performing products, profitable regions, customer purchasing patterns, and operational opportunities through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Main platform used for creating interactive reports and dashboards.
📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to create calculated measures, KPIs, and business metrics.
🔗 Data Modeling – Relationships established among sales-related tables for accurate reporting and analysis.
📈 Data Visualization – Interactive charts, maps, KPI cards, slicers, and trend analysis visuals.
📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard presentation.
4. Data Source

Source: Super Store Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales records including:

Order Sales
Profit
Quantity Sold
Customer Segment
Product Category & Sub-Category
Region & State Information
Shipping Mode
Payment Mode
Monthly Sales Performance

The data provides a complete view of business operations and customer purchasing behavior across different geographical locations.

5. Features & Highlights
🔹 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data daily, making it difficult to manually identify:

Which regions generate the highest revenue?
Which product categories contribute most to sales?
What customer segments drive business growth?
Which shipping methods are most preferred?
How do sales and profits fluctuate throughout the year?

Without a centralized reporting system, business leaders may struggle to make informed decisions quickly.
